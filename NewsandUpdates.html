Game & Music News

Stay updated with the latest releases and events.

Grand Theft Auto VI

GTA 6 is officially set for release in 2025! Set in Vice City, it promises an expansive open-world experience, a gripping storyline, and groundbreaking features that push the boundaries of the franchise.

New Carti Album Drop

Playboi Carti's highly anticipated album, I Am Music, is scheduled for release today, March 14, 2025. This marks his first album since 2020's Whole Lotta Red. The project has experienced several delays and title changes, initially announced as Narcissist in 2021 before adopting its current name.

Game Drop Delayed!

Assassin's Creed Shadows, initially scheduled for release on November 15, 2024, has experienced two delays. The first postponement moved the launch to February 14, 2025, allowing Ubisoft additional time for polishing, especially after insights from the Star Wars Outlaws launch. Subsequently, the release was further delayed to March 20, 2025, to incorporate player feedback and enhance the overall gaming experience.
