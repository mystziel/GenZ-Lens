Game Drop Delayed!

Assassin's Creed Shadows, initially scheduled for release on November 15, 2024, has experienced two delays. The first postponement moved the launch to February 14, 2025, allowing Ubisoft additional time for polishing, especially after insights from the Star Wars Outlaws launch. Subsequently, the release was further delayed to March 20, 2025, to incorporate player feedback and enhance the overall gaming experience.
